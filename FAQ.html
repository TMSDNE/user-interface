FAQ

Answers to common questions about what does and does not exist

Where do you get your data?

TMSDNE gets its data from a variety of sources on both the left and the right. Using some of the top financial journals' api output we collect our data daily.

Why would you want to take journal data out of context

In our humble estimation most financial journalism inherently is out of context. With the bias in all forms of media TMSDNE is an exercise in trying to leverage data science to create less bias financial journalism.

What other forms of journalism could this be applied to?

Theoretically, any type of journalism that needs to be leveled out. As the algorithm becomes stronger through deep learning, the articles would become less bias and more consumable.

If it doens't exist, what is it?

That is an existential question that we here at TMSDNE are not equipped to answer.
